About Butt Tis buuri Scholarship Fund

What you need to know about the Butt-Tis buuri Scholarship Fund

Our non-profit organization provides financial support for brilliant but needy students from Bawku West District in Ghana to access tertiary, vocational, and apprenticeship training programs. Our aim is to increase access to education, skills training, and financial services for young people who cannot pursue further education without support.

Know More

Scholarship Requirements

Eligibility

  • Applicants must come from the Kusaug Toende Area( Bawku West District) & be brilliant but needy.
  • Applicants must be Senior High/Technical school graduates & not enrolled in any tertiary institution.
  • Applicants must apply to any public Tertiary school in Ghana( University, Technical University or College ).
  • Those who do not qualify for tertiary education but are eager to acquire a skill and start a trade can apply as well.
  • The Applicant must be prepared to Come-Back and Give-Back to the Community.

Application Process

  • Interested students should download the application form here to apply.
  • Completed application form together with all documents should be submitted to the secretariat, Butt-tis Buuri Scholarship Fund, Zebilla.
  • Shortlisted applicants will be contacted.

Documents to attach:

  • Admission Letter
  • JHS/SHS/Vocational/Technical Certificate
  • Birth Certificate/Ghana Card/Passport
  • Other Supporting Documents

Please Note

  • Applying is completely FREE!
  • Applications submitted through any other means will not be accepted.
  • Applicant must not be on any other Government of Ghana scholarship or any State Institution scholarship.
  • All applicants shall complete an essay or statement of career objectives to justify their request for the scholarship.
  • Misrepresentation in any material form renders the application null and void. Award made based on that shall be withdrawn and person(s) involved prosecuted.
  • Only shortlisted applicants will be contacted.

Deadline

  • 30th April, 2023
