About Butt Tis buuri Scholarship Fund

We are a non-profit organization that provides financial support for brilliant but needy students from Bawku West District in Ghana to access tertiary, vocational, and apprenticeship training programs. Our aim is to increase access to education, skills training, and financial services for young people who cannot pursue further education without support.

Our Scholarship Fund was established on June 4th, 2022, by DR. JOHN KINGSLEY KRUGU to celebrate his birthday. After speaking with tertiary students from the Kusaug Toende area, Dr. Krugu saw how many of them were struggling to stay in school due to financial challenges. Drawing from his own experience of overcoming obstacles to access tertiary education, he decided to create this fund to help young people reach their full potential.

The Scholarship is an educational endowment that targets high/technical school graduates from all backgrounds. We provide students with an opportunity to attend two, three, four, or six-year tertiary/vocational schools or an apprenticeship program. We aim to empower students with academic or trade opportunities that align with their interests and motivate them to learn and grow. Our goal is to stimulate and nurture the physical, intellectual, creative, emotional, social, and moral development of each beneficiary student.

We believe that education is the key to empowering students to become productive citizens in their communities. Tertiary training, vocational training, and apprenticeships allow students to communicate, cooperate, and work with educators and employers to improve their future employment prospects. Our goal is to provide students with practical experiences that help develop positive attitudes toward being dependable, responsible, and cooperative individuals.

We are grateful for the support of DR. JOHN KINGSLEY KRUGU and other philanthropies and benevolent individuals and organizations who contribute to our cause. Together, we can help young people from Bawku West District access education and training opportunities that can transform their lives and contribute to the development of their communities.
